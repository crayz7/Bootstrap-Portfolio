About Me

I was born in Austin and have only lived in one other city, Houston, where I went to law school and worked as an attorney for several years. I graduated from The University of Texas at Austin with a Political Science degree and pursued law school a year later.

While Houston is a great city with almost limitless food possibilities, Austin is where my heart is. I love the vibe and outdoor activities such as hiking and biking. I also love (and hate) to golf and Austin is very scenic with hills and the beautiful Colorado River running through the city.
